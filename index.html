PHILOSOPHY

"Simplicity is the ultimate form of sophistication." - Leonardo Da Vinci

While we are tempted to build complex things, design can be forgotten later in the process. Design first and design less. As Antoine de Saint-Exupery puts it well, "perfection is achieved not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away."
